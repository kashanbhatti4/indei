Unveiling the Mechanisms and Processes Behind

Wondering how we achieve this level of accuracy? INDEINSIDER employs a sophisticated algorithm and AI to estimate Steam game sales and revenue using several robust methodologies:

  1. Tracking Steam Top Seller Rank

    We closely monitor the Steam top seller rank, utilizing this data to estimate revenue and sales for each game.

  2. Concurrent Player Tracking

    By analyzing the number of concurrent players, we estimate the game's player base, providing valuable insights into a game's popularity.

  3. Boxleiter Method Enhancement

    We use an advanced Boxleiter method incorporating regression analysis with factors like release year, review scores, playtimes, and more.

  4. Actual Units Sold Analysis

    Leveraging publicly disclosed data on actual units sold, we further refine our estimates, ensuring a high degree of accuracy.
